In your team application you have to implement the following tasks:
1 - Create a model of games that will provide records of games played by the team:
    game_date, opponent, score

    game_id, minute, player_id
3 - Provide some sample data either by using the shell or the db directly
4 - Create a page with a scorers list of players that have scored ordered by the number of goals
